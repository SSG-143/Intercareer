HTML/CSS Developer Internship

Lead India


Remote – India • Full-Time Internship – 3 Months • ₹16,000–₹17,000/month

About the Job


Lead India is looking for a creative and detail-oriented HTML/CSS Developer Intern to join our web development team. This internship offers a chance to gain hands-on experience in building responsive and visually appealing websites using core front-end technologies.


Key Responsibilities


  • Develop and maintain web pages using semantic HTML and modern CSS.
  • Translate design mockups into functional, responsive interfaces.
  • Ensure cross-browser compatibility and mobile responsiveness.
  • Collaborate with designers and developers to enhance UI/UX.
  • Optimize web pages for performance and accessibility.
  • Test and debug layout and styling issues.

Required Skills


  • Proficiency in HTML5 and CSS3.
  • Knowledge of responsive design & media queries.
  • Familiarity with Bootstrap or Tailwind (preferred).
  • Basic Git/version control skills.
  • Attention to detail and design accuracy.
  • Strong communication & problem-solving skills.

Eligibility


  • Pursuing or recently completed a degree in Computer Science, Web Design, or related field.
  • Portfolio or GitHub showcasing HTML/CSS projects (preferred).

What We Offer


  • Real-world front-end development experience.
  • Mentorship from experienced developers/designers.
  • Internship certificate upon completion.
  • Potential for full-time role based on performance.
