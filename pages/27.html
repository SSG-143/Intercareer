About the Internship


Engineer’s Cradle is offering a Data Entry Internship for students looking to enhance their administrative and operational skills. This role focuses on accurate data management, documentation, and organizational support in a remote working environment.


Key Responsibilities


  • Accurately input and update data into spreadsheets, internal systems, and databases.
  • Verify and correct any data discrepancies.
  • Maintain data confidentiality and integrity at all times.
  • Assist in preparing reports and summaries when required.
  • Organize and file digital documents systematically.
  • Support the team with administrative and operational tasks.

Requirements


  • Basic proficiency in MS Office (Excel, Word) or Google Workspace.
  • Strong attention to detail and accuracy.
  • Good typing speed and reliable data entry skills.
  • Ability to work independently and meet deadlines.
  • Strong organizational and time management abilities.

Internship Details


  • Type: Work From Home, Full-Time
