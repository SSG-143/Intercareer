Data Analytics Intern

DataVoyage


Remote • Internship • 6 Months

About the Internship


DataVoyage is offering a 6-month remote Data Analytics Internship for graduates and students eager to build careers in data science and analytics. This role provides valuable international exposure and helps you enhance your portfolio with practical, real-world projects.


Key Responsibilities


  • Analyze and interpret datasets to derive meaningful insights.
  • Support report preparation and dashboard generation.
  • Work with tools such as Excel, SQL, Python (Pandas), and Power BI/Tableau.
  • Contribute to data quality reviews and improvement activities.

Eligibility


  • Graduation between 2017–2022 (any background).
  • Basic knowledge of Excel, SQL, Python, or Power BI.
  • Strong interest in data and analytics with willingness to learn.
  • No prior experience required.

What You Will Receive


  • Internship certificate from a UK-registered company.
  • LinkedIn recommendation based on performance.
  • Exposure to remote international work culture.
  • Feedback and guidance to improve your data portfolio.

Internship Details


  • Location: Work From Home
  • Duration: 6 Months
  • Stipend: Unpaid (career development internship)
  • Working Days: 5 Days a Week
  • Application Deadline: 24 Aug 2025, 12:00 AM IST
  • Perks: Certificate of Completion, Flexible Hours
