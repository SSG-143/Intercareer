SEO & SMO Specialist

PixelMania4K


Rewa, Madhya Pradesh • Full-time • On-site

About the Company


PixelMania4K is a creative agency specializing in brand identity, graphic design, video editing, web services, photography, content writing, and social media management. We transform ideas into impactful visual and narrative experiences that engage audiences.


About the Role


The SEO & SMO Specialist will focus on enhancing online visibility by optimizing websites for search engines, managing social media campaigns, and developing strategies to boost engagement and reach. This is a full-time, on-site role in Rewa, Madhya Pradesh.


Key Responsibilities


  • Optimize websites to achieve higher search engine rankings.
  • Manage and grow social media platforms with engaging content.
  • Analyze website traffic and campaign performance using SEO tools.
  • Plan, implement, and analyze social media campaigns.
  • Develop strategies to strengthen online presence and visibility.

Qualifications & Skills


  • Strong knowledge of SEO, keyword research, and web analytics.
  • Experience in social media management and campaign analysis.
  • Proficiency with SEO tools like Google Analytics and SEMrush.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • Creative mindset, able to work in a fast-paced environment.
  • Bonus: Experience with web services, graphic design, or video editing.
  • Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Communications, or related field.
