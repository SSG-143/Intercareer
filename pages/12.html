About The Role


Want to empower small-town students and shape the next generation of career-ready talent? Join Thriversity as a Content & Students coordinator Intern and be part of a mission that truly matters!


About Thriversity


Thriversity is an employability and career-building ecosystem empowering students from Tier 2 & Tier 3 cities with practical skills in communication, confidence, personal branding, problem-solving, and more — bridging the gap between college and career.

  • Role: Content & Campus Engagement Intern
  • Mode: Hybrid
  • Duration: 12-16 weeks
  • Perks: Stipend | Internship Certificate | Founder Mentorship | Premium Content Access | Real Exposure

What You'll Do:


Content Creation – Co-create learning content (posts, reels, slides, tool-kits) that addresses real student needs.

Campus Engagement – Leverage your peer network to spread Thriversity’s mission and bring students onboard.

Learning Session Support – Assist in planning, moderating, and hosting engaging webinars and workshops.

Ecosystem Building – Build partnerships with student clubs, placement cells, and faculty.

Student Needs Discovery – Interact with students to understand career challenges and shape future programs.


Who Can Apply:


Current students or recent graduates from any stream Passionate about helping others grow, especially from underexposed backgrounds.

Strong communication skills & content creation ability Comfortable with Google Docs, Canva, Instagram, and WhatsApp

Bonus: Experience in reels, design, campus campaigns, or managing events
