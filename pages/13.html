Front-End Developer Intern

Lead India


Remote (India) • Full-time • 3 Months • ₹16,000–₹17,000/month

About The Role


Lead India is looking for a motivated and detail-oriented Front-End Developer Intern to join our technology team. This internship offers hands-on experience in building responsive and user-friendly web interfaces that support our mission-driven initiatives.


Key Responsibilities


  • Develop and maintain web pages using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Collaborate with designers and back-end developers to implement UI/UX designs
  • Ensure cross-browser compatibility and mobile responsiveness
  • Optimize web applications for performance and scalability
  • Debug and troubleshoot front-end issues
  • Stay updated with emerging front-end technologies and best practices

Required Skills


  • Basic proficiency in HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript
  • Familiarity with front-end frameworks such as React, Angular, or Vue (preferred but not required)
  • Understanding of responsive design and accessibility principles
  • Ability to translate design mockups into functional web pages
  • Strong problem-solving and communication skills
  • Willingness to learn and adapt in a collaborative environment

Eligibility


  • Currently pursuing or recently completed a degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field
  • Portfolio or GitHub projects demonstrating front-end work (preferred)

What We Offer


  • Practical experience in front-end development and UI/UX implementation
  • Mentorship from experienced developers
  • Internship Certificate upon successful completion
  • Potential for full-time employment based on performance

If you're interested in this opportunity, please submit your application including your resume and portfolio/GitHub links.
