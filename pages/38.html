Search Engine Optimization Specialist

Tiramisu New Media Solutions


Bengaluru, Karnataka • Full-time • Hybrid

About the Company


Tiramisu New Media Solutions is a Bengaluru-based digital marketing agency specializing in website development, SEO, and social media traffic growth. We leverage modern tools to help businesses expand their reach while maintaining their brand identity.


About the Role


The SEO Specialist will focus on driving online visibility through keyword research, SEO audits, on-page optimization, link building, and data analysis. This is a hybrid role based in Bengaluru with flexibility for remote work.


Key Responsibilities


  • Conduct keyword research to identify opportunities for growth.
  • Perform SEO audits and recommend improvements.
  • Build quality backlinks to strengthen domain authority.
  • Optimize on-page SEO including metadata, content, and structure.
  • Analyze website traffic and performance using SEO tools.

Qualifications & Skills


  • Experience in keyword research, link building, and SEO audits.
  • Strong knowledge of web analytics and data interpretation.
  • Hands-on experience with Google Analytics and Search Console.
  • Excellent attention to detail and task prioritization skills.
  • Strong written and verbal communication abilities.
  • Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Computer Science, or related field (preferred).
