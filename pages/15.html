Junior Software Engineer

Cornerstone


Office-based – Pune, India • Full-time

About the Company


Cornerstone is a global leader in AI-powered workforce agility solutions, empowering over 7,000 organizations and more than 100 million users across 180+ countries. Our platform, Cornerstone Galaxy, helps businesses identify skills gaps, retain top talent, and deliver world-class learning experiences in multiple languages.


About the Role


We are seeking a motivated Junior Software Engineer to join our Pune office. You will design, develop, and deliver scalable software solutions for leading organizations worldwide. Working in an agile development framework, you will collaborate closely with the team to meet user needs and take ownership of feature delivery from concept to deployment.


Key Responsibilities


  • Analyze specifications and design solutions in line with established standards.
  • Identify, troubleshoot, and resolve moderately complex software issues.
  • Write clean, testable code and create unit tests for new features.
  • Participate in team code reviews to ensure quality and consistency.
  • Collaborate with support, services, and product management teams when needed.
  • Assist in estimating deliverables and join requirement/design review meetings.

Qualifications


  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field.
  • Fresh graduates with a strong eagerness to learn are welcome.
  • Proficiency in Core and Advanced Java or .NET.
  • Good understanding of web technologies and object-oriented programming.
  • Experience with GitHub, Bitbucket, or similar version control tools.
  • Knowledge of React or similar frameworks is a plus.
  • Basic SQL and RDBMS knowledge preferred.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills.
  • Familiarity with Agile/Scrum methodologies.

Our Culture


At Cornerstone, we believe in sparking greatness, breaking boundaries, and sharing success. We value curiosity, innovation, and collaboration to help individuals and organizations thrive in the evolving world of work.
