Content Creator Intern

Prophive Realty


Bengaluru, Karnataka • Internship • On-site

About the Role


Prophive Realty is looking for a Content Creator Intern to join their Bengaluru team. This paid internship is ideal for students with strong communication skills who are comfortable on camera. The intern will be responsible for creating engaging content, managing social media, and assisting in digital marketing campaigns.


Key Responsibilities


  • Develop and design engaging content across multiple platforms.
  • Create graphics and visuals to support campaigns.
  • Manage and schedule posts for social media platforms.
  • Collaborate with the marketing team to execute content strategies.
  • Assist in digital campaigns and brand promotion activities.

Qualifications & Skills


  • Experience in content creation and social media management.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • Ability to collaborate and work in a team environment.
  • Bachelor’s degree or currently pursuing Marketing, Communications, Graphic Design, or related fields.
  • Knowledge of digital marketing strategies (preferred).
  • Prior internship experience in content creation (preferred).
  • Comfortable on camera (preferred).

Internship Details


  • Location: Bengaluru, Karnataka (On-site)
  • Type: Paid Internship
  • Eligibility: Open to students of any colleges in Bengaluru
