About the Internship


This internship is a great opportunity to explore the core areas of marketing — from market research and audience segmentation to campaign implementation and collaboration with stakeholders. By the end of this role, you’ll have hands-on experience in building and executing data-driven marketing strategies.


Key Learning Outcomes


  • Conduct in-depth market research and create detailed customer profiles.
  • Segment audiences and prepare targeted lists using data enrichment tools.
  • Produce engaging content for social media and websites.
  • Execute and track marketing campaigns across channels like email and LinkedIn.
  • Analyze large datasets to extract actionable marketing insights.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to meet business objectives.

Requirements


  • Strong online research skills.
  • Ability to work both independently and collaboratively.
  • Genuine interest in marketing and related fields.
  • Curiosity and eagerness to learn quickly.
