Fraud Investigation Associate

Slice


Bengaluru, Karnataka(On-site) • Full-time • 1–3 Years Experience

About the Team


The Fraud Investigation team at slice ensures the safety of products and customers by investigating suspicious activities, identifying fraud patterns, and preventing financial losses. If problem-solving, pattern detection, and quick decision-making come naturally to you, this role is a perfect fit.


About the Role


As a Fraud Investigation Associate, you will investigate fraud incidents, analyze patterns, and coordinate with multiple teams to strengthen fraud detection. This role requires sharp decision-making, adaptability to startup culture, and excellent communication skills.


Key Responsibilities


  • Investigate fraud activity on slice products and respond quickly to incidents.
  • Review fraud alerts daily and take preventive action.
  • Identify fraud patterns and share insights with the analytics team.
  • Collaborate with analytics, product, and other teams to prevent losses.
  • Prepare investigation reports with accuracy and timeliness.
  • Recommend tools and methods to improve fraud detection efficiency.

Qualifications & Skills


  • 1–3 years of experience in fraud operations/investigation (Banking or Fintech).
  • Graduate degree in a relevant field.
  • Fluent in English & Hindi, with good communication skills.
  • Proficiency in Excel and basic computer knowledge.
  • Strong judgment, decision-making, and pattern recognition abilities.
  • Presentable and comfortable speaking with customers during investigations.

Life at slice


  • Competitive salaries and medical insurance (for employees & dependents).
  • Flexible working hours & tailored leave policies.
  • Learning, upskilling, and reward opportunities.
  • Friendly work culture with games, food, and fun activities.
