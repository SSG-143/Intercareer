Marketing & Brand Management Internship

Mindenious


Remote • Full-Time • 1 Month

Eligibility


  • Engineering Students
  • MBA Students
  • Postgraduate Students
  • Undergraduate Students

About the Internship


This internship offers the opportunity to work on diverse marketing and branding initiatives. You will engage in market research, campaign planning, and brand management while building skills that are highly relevant for a career in marketing.


Key Responsibilities


  • Conduct market research to identify trends, opportunities, and target audiences.
  • Assist in creating and executing multi-channel marketing strategies.
  • Produce engaging content for social media, email campaigns, and promotional materials.
  • Support lead generation and nurturing activities.
  • Contribute to brand-building and brand-management efforts.
  • Analyze campaign performance and provide improvement recommendations.

Requirements


  • Strong skills in market research and analysis.
  • Understanding of marketing strategy fundamentals.
  • Good communication and interpersonal abilities.
  • Familiarity with brand management practices.
  • Ability to work independently and in a team.
  • Analytical thinking and problem-solving skills.
  • Quick learner with adaptability to new challenges.

Internship Details


  • Type: Work From Home, Full-Time

  • Work Days: 5 Days a Week
  • Application Deadline: 18 Aug 2025, 08:10 AM IST

Perks

  • Certificate of Completion
  • Letter of Recommendation
