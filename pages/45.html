Associate Cybersecurity Engineer (IAM)

Visa


Bengaluru, Karnataka, India • Full-Time • Hybrid

About the Job


Visa is a global leader in payments and technology, enabling secure and seamless transactions across 200+ countries. As an Associate Cybersecurity Engineer in the Identity and Access Management (IAM) team, you will play a key role in securing Visa’s systems through automation, privileged access management, governance, and AI-driven identity solutions.


Key Responsibilities


  • Operate and optimize IAM solutions including SailPoint, ForgeRock, Okta, Azure AD, and CyberArk.
  • Manage identity lifecycle: provisioning, authentication, and privileged access.
  • Automate user access, certifications, and compliance with Python, PowerShell, and APIs.
  • Implement PAM solutions with secure credential vaulting and session management.
  • Develop AI/ML-driven analytics for anomaly detection and role mining.
  • Ensure IAM compliance with GDPR, SOX, PCI-DSS, and HIPAA standards.
  • Investigate IAM-related incidents and enforce Zero Trust principles.

Qualifications & Skills


  • Bachelor’s degree or 3+ years of relevant cybersecurity/IAM experience.
  • Hands-on knowledge of IAM tools (SailPoint, Okta, CyberArk, BeyondTrust, Azure AD).
  • Understanding of Active Directory, LDAP, SAML, OAuth, and OIDC.
  • Scripting experience in Python, PowerShell, or Java for IAM automation.
  • Familiarity with AI/ML and Gen AI for IAM use cases.
  • Strong understanding of Zero Trust, least privilege, and identity governance.
  • Relevant certifications (CISSP, CISA, CISM, IAM vendor certs) are a plus.

Job Details


  • Location: Bengaluru, Karnataka, India
  • Type: Full-Time
  • Mode: Hybrid

Join Visa’s Cybersecurity IAM team and be part of building secure, scalable, and innovative identity solutions that protect millions of users and enable global financial growth.
