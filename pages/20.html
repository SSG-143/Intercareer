International Business Development Internship

Retrotech Business Solutions Pvt. Ltd.


On-site Gurgaon • Full-Time Internship • Monday–Friday

About the Company


Retrotech Business Solutions is a global leader in refurbishing and supplying used IT assets to customers worldwide. With a strong focus on sustainability, we ensure that valuable hardware like laptops and desktops get a second life instead of being discarded — reducing waste and conserving resources.


About the Role


We’re looking for enthusiastic interns for our International Business Development team. You’ll explore and build global business relationships, particularly in IT hardware equipment markets, and contribute to expanding our presence in countries like the USA, UK, UAE, and across Europe.


Post Internship


  • Salary: ₹28,000/month (based on performance).
  • Full-Time Opportunity: Yes.
  • Work Days: Monday–Friday (Last Saturday of the month for training).

Key Responsibilities


  • Identify and pursue new international business opportunities via networking and social media.
  • Conduct online research, email marketing, and social media campaigns to discover potential partners.
  • Negotiate and build strong relationships with international clients.
  • Analyze market trends and develop strategies to improve procurement and sales.
  • Collaborate with the team to meet and exceed business goals.
  • Collect feedback from clients and share insights with internal teams.

Benefits


  • 5-day work week in a fast-paced, growth-oriented environment.
  • International exposure working with global counterparts.
  • Attractive incentive programs and recognition rewards.
  • Medical insurance coverage.
  • Personal development and training programs.
  • Work-life balance and an inclusive, diverse workplace.
  • Clear career growth opportunities with mentorship support.
