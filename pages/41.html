Analog Circuit Design Intern

Siemens Technology & Services Pvt Ltd


Bengaluru (On-site) • Internship (Contract) • 6 Months

About the Role


Siemens Technology & Services Pvt Ltd is offering a 6-month on-site internship for an Analog Circuit Design Intern in Bengaluru. The intern will assist in designing, testing, and analyzing electronic circuits and systems while working closely with the engineering team.


Key Responsibilities


  • Assist in designing and developing new electronic circuits.
  • Test and troubleshoot existing circuit designs.
  • Collaborate with engineers to improve system performance.
  • Support circuit analysis and simulation tasks.

Qualifications & Skills


  • Knowledge of hardware design flow (circuit simulation, schematic capture, layout).
  • Familiarity with microcontroller architectures (ARM, AVR, MSP430, 8051, etc.).
  • Experience in electronic circuit design using Op-Amps, ADCs, DACs.
  • Understanding of discrete semiconductors (BJT, MOSFET, Diodes).
  • Strong grasp of passive components (resistors, capacitors, inductors).

Internship Details


  • Location: On-site (Bengaluru)
  • Duration: 6 Months
  • Type: Contract Internship
