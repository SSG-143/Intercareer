Junior SDET

Betterworks


Bangalore, Karnataka, India • Engineering • Full-Time • Entry Level

About Betterworks


Betterworks provides enterprise software that simplifies strategic planning, OKRs, and performance conversations. Trusted by leading brands like Walmart and Intuit, Betterworks empowers organizations to align goals, engage employees, and execute effectively with Continuous Performance Management® solutions.


Role Overview


As a Junior Software Development Engineer in Test (SDET), you will contribute to ensuring product quality through manual and automated testing. This entry-level position offers the opportunity to work with automation tools, CI/CD pipelines, and Agile teams while building a career in software quality engineering.


Key Responsibilities


  • Identify risks and evaluate product quality across releases.
  • Design and write manual and automated test cases.
  • Support test automation using Cypress.io and integrate suites into CI/CD pipelines.
  • Help define quality metrics and maintain QA documentation.
  • Review developer implementations and highlight issues before release.
  • Assist in planning test cycles, creating test data, and preparing quality reports.

Qualifications & Skills


  • 0–1 years of experience in QA and automation.
  • Strong understanding of manual testing principles.
  • Basic debugging knowledge for web applications.
  • Understanding of REST APIs, databases, sessions, and tokens.
  • Exposure to Scrum and Agile practices.
  • Experience with Cypress.io, Selenium, or similar tools (projects, coursework, or internships acceptable).
  • Attention to detail and proactive communication.
  • Performance and security testing knowledge (a plus).

Life at Betterworks


  • Remote-first culture with flexibility to work from anywhere in India.
  • Unlimited vacation and work-life balance.
  • Team events and vibrant company culture.
  • Competitive salary with stock options.
  • Health and personal accident insurance coverage.
  • One-time home office setup reimbursement and monthly internet stipend.
