Marketing Intern

Mindenious


Remote • Internship • 1 Month

About the Internship


Mindenious is offering a 1-month remote Marketing Internship designed for students and graduates eager to build their skills in market research, content creation, and brand strategy. This role provides practical exposure to multiple aspects of digital marketing while working from home.


Key Responsibilities


  • Conduct market research and analysis to identify trends, opportunities, and target audiences.
  • Assist in developing and implementing marketing strategies across different channels.
  • Create engaging content for social media, email campaigns, and other marketing materials.
  • Participate in lead generation and lead nurturing activities to support conversions.
  • Contribute to brand-building and brand management initiatives.
  • Analyze marketing campaign performance and provide actionable insights.

Qualifications & Skills


  • Knowledge of market research and analysis techniques.
  • Understanding of marketing strategies and brand management concepts.
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Analytical and problem-solving abilities.
  • Ability to work independently as well as in a team environment.
  • Quick learner with adaptability to new challenges.

Internship Details


  • Location: Work From Home
  • Duration: 1 Month
  • Stipend: ₹1,000 – ₹10,000 / Month
  • Working Days: 5 Days a Week
  • Application Deadline: 21 Aug 2025, 02:40 AM IST
  • Perks: Job Offer, Certificate of Completion, Letter of Recommendation
