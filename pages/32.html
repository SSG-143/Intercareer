Social Media Marketing Intern

DIGITAL EXPRESS TECHNOLOGIES


Remote • Internship • 6 Months

About the Internship


DIGITAL EXPRESS TECHNOLOGIES is offering a 6-month Social Media Marketing Internship for motivated and creative individuals. This program is designed for freshers and early-career professionals (0–2 years of experience) who are enthusiastic about social media and digital marketing. Interns will gain hands-on experience in live projects while working remotely from anywhere in India.


Key Responsibilities


  • Create engaging social media content and manage posts on Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn.
  • Identify and target industries in need of digital transformation through lead generation.
  • Conduct outreach on LinkedIn, Facebook, and Instagram to connect with potential clients.
  • Nurture leads and contribute to lead conversion into qualified prospects.

Qualifications & Skills


  • Bachelor’s Degree, BBA, or MBA (students and graduates are eligible).
  • 0–2 years of experience in social media or digital marketing.
  • Creative mindset with basic design skills and strong communication abilities.
  • Tech-savvy, quick learner, and proactive in approach.

Internship Details


  • Location: Work From Home (Remote, India)
  • Duration: 6 Months
  • Openings: 15
  • Compensation: Unpaid, with 10% commission on each order achieved
  • Certification: Certificate of Completion provided at the end

By joining DIGITAL EXPRESS TECHNOLOGIES, you’ll gain exposure to diverse domains such as Web Development, Mobile App Development, Digital Marketing Automation, SEO, and AI Solutions. While headquartered in Ahmedabad & Hyderabad, this role offers full flexibility to work from anywhere in India.
