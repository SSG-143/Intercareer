Data Analyst Intern

Native Engineering Solutions


Remote • Internship • 3 Months

About the Internship


Native Engineering Solutions is hiring Data Analyst Interns to work on real-world datasets and contribute to meaningful business insights. This internship offers hands-on exposure to data collection, analysis, visualization, and reporting while working remotely.


Key Responsibilities


  • Collect, clean, and organize raw data from various sources.
  • Use Excel, Google Sheets, or SQL to analyze datasets and extract insights.
  • Create dashboards and reports with Power BI, Tableau, or Google Data Studio.
  • Collaborate with teams to understand data requirements and provide solutions.
  • Identify trends, patterns, and outliers to optimize business processes.
  • Ensure data accuracy, consistency, and integrity.
  • Present findings clearly to both technical and non-technical teams.

Qualifications & Skills


  • Engineering, Undergraduate, or Postgraduate students eligible.
  • Knowledge of SQL, Excel, and Google Sheets.
  • Experience with visualization tools like Tableau, Power BI, or Google Data Studio.
  • Strong analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills.
  • Ability to work independently and collaboratively in remote setups.

Internship Details


  • Location: Work From Home
  • Duration: 3 Months
  • Stipend: ₹22,000 / Month
  • Working Days: 5 Days a Week
  • Application Deadline: 28 Aug 2025, 12:00 AM IST
  • Perks: Job Offer, Certificate of Completion, Letter of Recommendation
