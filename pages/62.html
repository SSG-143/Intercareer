FAQs

Is this a full-time role?
No, this is a 24-month apprenticeship program, not a permanent position.

Is relocation support provided?
Yes, if you relocate more than 100 km to the assigned office.

Do I need prior experience?
Less than 1 year of data analytics experience is required. Fresh graduates are encouraged to apply.

Will I get a certificate?
Yes, on successful completion of the full program, apprentices receive a certificate of completion.

Can this lead to a full-time job?
Yes, apprentices may apply for Google FTE roles during the final 3 months of the program.
