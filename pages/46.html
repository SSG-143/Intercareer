DeAngelis Diamond


On-Site • Internship / Co-Op • Naples, Florida

About the Company


DeAngelis Diamond is an award-winning, ENR Top 400 national construction management firm specializing in healthcare, commercial, and multi-family projects. Headquartered in Naples, Florida, the company fosters innovation, collaboration, and excellence in construction.


About the Internship


This program provides students with real-world experience in Project Management, Field Operations, and Preconstruction. Interns will gain hands-on training while contributing to ongoing projects under the guidance of industry leaders.


Responsibilities


  • Office Interns (Precon & PM): Work on estimating, subcontractor solicitation, bid reviews, contracts, RFIs, pay applications, schedules, and project closeout tasks.
  • Field Interns: Assist with safety oversight, quality control, subcontractor coordination, scheduling, scope reviews, RFIs, punch lists, and closeout processes.

Qualifications


  • Pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Construction Management, Civil Engineering, or related field.
  • Passion for construction and technology-driven innovation.
  • Strong communication, problem-solving, and teamwork skills.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite and construction tools (Procore, Bluebeam, Plangrid, etc.).

Perks & Benefits


  • Competitive compensation with weekly pay schedule.
  • 11 paid holidays + birthday off + Veterans Day off (for veterans).
  • Volunteer time off + housing allowance + cell phone stipend.
  • DD team apparel allowance + free snacks & drinks.

Note: Drug test, background check, and eligibility to work in the U.S. are required.
