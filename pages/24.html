Product Marketing Internship

Mindenious


Remote • Full-Time • 1 Month

Eligibility


  • Engineering Students
  • MBA Students
  • Postgraduate Students

About the Internship


This is a great opportunity for students and recent graduates to gain hands-on experience in marketing. You’ll assist in market research, help develop strategies, and contribute to marketing campaigns aimed at promoting products and services across digital platforms.


Key Responsibilities


  • Conduct market research to identify trends, opportunities, and customer insights.
  • Assist in developing and executing marketing strategies.
  • Create marketing materials for social media, websites, and email campaigns.
  • Analyze campaign performance and suggest improvements.
  • Participate in lead generation and engagement activities.

Requirements


  • Basic understanding of marketing principles and concepts.
  • Strong market research and analytical skills.
  • Familiarity with digital marketing channels.
  • Good communication and collaboration skills.
  • Self-motivated and able to manage time effectively in a remote setup.
  • Excellent writing and speaking skills.
  • Ability to work both independently and in a team.

Internship Details


  • Type: Work From Home, Full-Time
  • Duration: 1 Month
  • Stipend: ₹1,000 – ₹10,000/month
  • Work Days: 5 Days a Week
  • Application Deadline: 18 Aug 2025, 08:19 AM IST

Perks

  • Certificate of Completion
  • Letter of Recommendation
