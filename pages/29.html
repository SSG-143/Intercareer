Full stack developer Intern

EduUdr India


Remote • Unpaid Internship • Flexible Duration

About the Company


EduUdr India is a free online learning and upskilling platform dedicated to making education accessible for learners of all backgrounds. The platform provides resources to help students and professionals improve their skills and knowledge, offering a user-friendly experience designed for continuous growth.


About the Role


This is a remote internship opportunity where interns will assist in content development, full-stack web projects, research activities, and digital marketing. Interns will also engage with learners, support educational initiatives, and contribute to the platform’s growth. Daily responsibilities will include creating and editing learning materials, attending team meetings, and providing support for ongoing projects.


Key Responsibilities


  • Create, edit, and manage educational learning content.
  • Assist in full-stack web development tasks and improvements.
  • Conduct research to support content and platform growth.
  • Support digital marketing and social media engagement strategies.
  • Participate in team discussions and provide project support.

Qualifications


  • Basic knowledge of computer programming.
  • Understanding of digital marketing and social media engagement.
  • Good communication and teamwork abilities.
  • Strong organizational skills and attention to detail.
  • Motivation and willingness to learn new skills.
  • Enrolled in or recently graduated from a relevant degree program.
