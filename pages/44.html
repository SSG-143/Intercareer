HR Intern

Allianz Services


Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala(On-site) • Internship • 3–6 Months

About the Internship


Allianz Services India is seeking a proactive and smart HR Intern to join its Trivandrum office. This role is an excellent opportunity for MBA HR students or graduates to gain practical HR experience through hands-on responsibilities and in-office collaboration.


Key Responsibilities


  • Coordinate and support HR events and activities.
  • Manage and analyze HR data using Excel and other tools.
  • Provide administrative support to the HR team.

Qualifications & Skills


  • MBA HR / Any Graduation or related field.
  • Strong organizational skills and attention to detail.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel and data management.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Good command of MS Office (Excel, PowerPoint, Word).
  • Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a fast-paced environment.

Internship Details


  • Location: Trivandrum (on-site, candidates from Trivandrum preferred)
  • Duration: 3–6 Months
  • Mode: In-office
