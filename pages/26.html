Mindenious


Remote • Part-Time • 1 Month

Eligibility

  • Postgraduate Students

About the Internship


This internship offers hands-on experience in leveraging WhatsApp as a marketing channel. You’ll develop campaigns, create engaging content, and manage customer engagement while learning data-driven optimization techniques.


Key Responsibilities


  • Plan and execute WhatsApp marketing campaigns to build brand awareness and engagement.
  • Create engaging content in text, image, and video formats optimized for WhatsApp.
  • Grow and manage WhatsApp contact lists using effective acquisition strategies.
  • Analyze campaign results and provide actionable insights.
  • Research target audiences and trends to refine marketing efforts.
  • Collaborate with the team to align WhatsApp strategies with overall marketing goals.
  • Respond to customer inquiries and gather feedback via WhatsApp.

Requirements


  • Understanding of marketing principles and strategies.
  • Excellent communication and content creation skills.
  • Creativity in producing engaging text, images, and video content.
  • Analytical mindset for tracking and improving campaign performance.
  • Knowledge of WhatsApp Business features.
  • Strong interpersonal skills and ability to work independently or in a team.
  • Organized, proactive, and results-oriented.

  • Work Days: 4 Days a Week
  • Application Deadline: 22 Aug 2025, 12:00 AM IST
  • Flexible Hours: Yes

Perks
