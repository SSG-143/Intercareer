Sales & Business Development Internship

Alexa Farms Pvt. Ltd.


Indore • On-site • Full-Time

About the Internship


We are seeking motivated and results-driven interns to join our Sales & Business Development team in Indore. This role offers hands-on experience in lead generation, client communication, and sales strategy — perfect for individuals looking to build a career in business growth and relationship management.


Key Responsibilities


  • Assist in generating leads via cold calls, email campaigns, and other outreach methods.
  • Follow up with potential clients and manage ongoing communications.
  • Prepare and present proposals and sales presentations.
  • Maintain accurate client records in the CRM system.
  • Work with the team to develop and execute business development strategies.
  • Analyze sales data to identify trends and improve performance.

Requirements


  • Strong sales and negotiation skills.
  • Excellent verbal and written communication abilities.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel for reporting and analysis.
  • Ability to confidently perform cold calling and lead follow-ups.
  • Familiarity with CRM tools is a plus.
  • Strong problem-solving and analytical skills.
  • Ability to work independently and within a team.
  • Excellent time management and organizational skills.

Internship Details


  • Location: Indore
  • Duration: 3 Months
  • Stipend: ₹5,000/month + Performance Incentives (up to 2.4 LPA)
  • Work Days: 5 Days a Week
  • Type: In-Office, Full-Time

Perks


  • Full-time job offer upon successful completion.
  • Certificate of Completion.
  • Letter of Recommendation.
