Pentester Intern — Application & Mobile Security

Appknox


Remote • Internship • Paid • PPO (top interns)

About the Company

Appknox is a leading mobile application security company (recognized by Gartner & G2). A profitable B2B SaaS startup headquartered in Singapore with operations in Bengaluru, Appknox helps businesses and mobile developers secure their apps with fast, high-quality security audits. We’ve secured apps for Fortune 500 companies and 300+ enterprises worldwide.


The Opportunity

Join the security team to perform end-to-end security audits, engage with clients, research new vulnerabilities to add to the scanner, and present research at conferences. This internship is ideal for students or recent grads keen on hands-on application/mobile security experience.


What an Ideal Candidate Looks Like

  • Pursuing or recently completed graduation/post-graduation in IT/security-related fields.
  • Skills in application penetration testing; IoT testing and source-code audits are plus points.
  • Comfortable interacting with clients (scoping calls, remediation calls).

Minimum Requirements

  • Comfortable with tools like Burp Suite, nmap, sqlmap, r2, etc.
  • Strong analytical skills and fundamentals of information security.
  • Strong grasp of Web, API and Mobile penetration testing.
  • Ability to produce accurate PoCs and clear write-ups for findings.
  • Basic understanding of cloud platforms (AWS/GCP) — security knowledge is a plus.
  • Self-driven learner who keeps up with new techniques and tools.

Responsibilities

  • Perform security assessments of web and mobile applications.
  • Explain findings with business impact and compliance implications.
  • Create PoCs and remediation guidance for identified vulnerabilities.
  • Continuously upskill on latest tools, techniques and attack surfaces.

Personality Traits We Admire

  • Confident, curious, and collaborative — sense of humour is welcome.
  • Attention to detail and the ability to find edge cases.
  • Proactive with fresh ideas and commitment to deadlines.
  • Willingness to ask questions and drive process improvements.

Interview Process

  • Round 1 — CTF Round: profile & skill evaluation.
  • Round 2 — Technical interview with a security team member.
  • Round 3 — Technical interview with Team Lead.
  • Round 4 — HR round.

Why Join Us

  • Competitive stipend and PPO opportunities for top interns.
  • Freedom & responsibility — ownership of real security projects.
  • Holistic growth with training and learning support.
  • Transparent startup culture and open communication.

Skills

Vulnerability assessment, penetration testing, vulnerability scanning, information security, web application security, mobile security, mobile app testing (QA).


FAQs

Q: Is this role paid?
Yes — this is a paid internship and the company offers stipend packages aligned with market standards.

Q: Do interns get PPO?
Top-performing interns may be offered PPO (pre-placement offer).

Q: Will I work with clients?
Yes — interns will engage with clients for scoping and remediation calls as part of audits.

Q: Is cloud security required?
Basic understanding of AWS/GCP is useful; deep cloud security experience is a plus but not mandatory.
